Get To Know Us

IFY is an idea which was birthed in a consultative meeting with the JWC Teenage class facilitators where we noted a disconnect after the teenagers graduate to join the Youth Ministry caused by lack of proper integration strategies and vibrant team to usher and guide them to be effective in their new stage. This resulted to the birth of the idea which I proposed to the team and I believe will prove beneficial. The ministry strategy has been applied in several churches including CITAM and Grace hill Mission ChurchKikuyu which has a very effective and functional structure.
